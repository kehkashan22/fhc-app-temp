About Us

Farooq Haque Classes ( A unit of YO Edutech Solutions Pvt. Ltd.)

Founded in 1998, by a professional on a mission to revolutionize the process of teaching prevalent in the country, Farooq Haque Classes ever since has endeavoured to blend innovative learning techniques and advanced information and communication technologies to offer modern, scalable and affordable solutions to the education community. Main objective being on delivering the best quality education to students pursuing CA / CS and CMA at their doorsteps from the best faculties across India.

Our primary focus is on overall development of the student through :

  • Convenience- Lectures available on DVD /USB and Android.
  • Clearing concepts and learning the practical application knowledge
  • Best quality notes for study and reference
  • Continuous updates of all Amendments, Case laws, Notifications, etc…
  • Improving Mind Power Skills
  • Study Plan
  • Time Management
  • Ease of communication – Presence across all Social Media Platforms

Our Objective:

To ensure that no matter who you are or where you are, you should not be deprived of best of education.

About CA Farooq Haque

“I can't change the direction of the wind, but I can adjust your sails to make you reach your destination”

Teaching is an art and it came very naturally to him. What started as a student tutoring his own classmates while pursuing chartered accountancy, ended up in revolutionizing the Indian education industry by establishing Farooq Haque Classes. He did not want to get lost in the crowd and that’s what he did. He followed his passion for teaching and wrote history.

With his deep understanding of the subjects, passion, humane and simple approach towards students and teacher fraternity and his resolve to give back to the society, made him extremely popular all over the country. He now has taught more than 35000 students.

Students have not only excelled in their academics ( highest being 1st All India Rank in CA Final) but also in their professional lives. Large numbers of his students are placed in top managerial positions in Indian and Multinational companies all across the globe.

To live up to his reputation as a perfectionist, he is constantly upgrading teaching techniques as well as education delivery modules so as to achieve his vision “To provide access to best quality Online & Offline Education beyond Geographical & Economic boundaries.”
