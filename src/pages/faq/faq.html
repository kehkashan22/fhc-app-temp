    Quiz Library

    Questions in CA exams test 3 basic skills.

  • Memory – The content for your answers depend directly on how much you remember. Specifically important words of law, section numbers etc.
  • Application – Many questions require you to apply the laws to give situation and judge, decide, interpret, or advice. In simple words, they test your application skills.
  • Speed- One need to think, contemplate, frame, conceptualize and write full answers with explanation within 3 hours.

    • In order to score high, one has to master all these skills apart from learning and developing in depth understanding of the subjects with help of FHC video lectures.

    And this is possible only if you practice writing answers regularly, which eats up lot of time.

    We at FHC have to developed these quizzes, to help you master all these skills with pinpoint accuracy in shortest possible time and most importantly it will prove to be so engaging that you will fall in love with the subject and change the way you learn forever. Instead of learning based approach you’ll start following performance based approach.
