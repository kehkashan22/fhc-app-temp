How to Study?

Effective study is an elusive and complex process involving various steps, requiring application of certain skills in the most optimized manner. At FHC we have developed a series of videos and tests that will help you experience the difference between "study" and "effective study".

About the Test

  • You will first see a video of a certain topic, explained in a straightforward, bland manner, and will be required to give a test on that topic.
  • After the first quiz, you will again be faced with another video on the same topic, this time explained in the unique style of Farooq Sir.
  • Once this video is over, another test will be presented.
  • Finally you will receive a comparison of the two scores you got with a customized analysis.

Just follow the unique tips given by Farooq Sir and use the quizzes and videos given in this app to further strengthen your skills.

All this will help you score high and reach your targets with ease.
